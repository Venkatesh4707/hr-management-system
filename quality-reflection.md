# Quality Reflection

## Do I write tests?

Previously, I focused mainly on building projects and manual verification. Through this HRMS project I learned the importance of automated testing and regression suites. I am developing the habit of writing tests before considering a feature complete.

---

## A bug I missed

While testing employee creation, I discovered that zero and negative salaries were accepted. This should have been prevented earlier. I learned that business rules are as important as technical correctness.

---

## One thing this team needs most

Automated tests.

They provide confidence and prevent repeated defects.

---

## Convincing a senior developer

I would start with a few fast tests protecting payroll and authentication. Showing reduced regressions is more effective than arguing.

---

## Personal System

During my GATE preparation and engineering projects, I relied on checklists and routines. I learned that simple systems people actually follow are better than complex systems nobody uses. I apply the same idea to software quality.
