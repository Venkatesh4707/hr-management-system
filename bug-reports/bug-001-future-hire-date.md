# Future Hire Date Accepted

## Severity
High

## Module
Employees

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Login as Super Root.
2. Go to Employees → Add Employee.
3. Enter a future hire date.
4. Fill all required fields.
5. Click Create Employee.

## Expected Result

Future hire dates should not be accepted.

## Actual Result

Employee is created successfully.

## Impact

Incorrect employee records can be created and affect payroll and reporting.
